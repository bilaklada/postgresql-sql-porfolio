# Findings

This project identifies the highest-revenue customers and separates customer
value from recency. That distinction matters because a high lifetime spender is
not automatically an active customer.

## What I would look for in the output

- customers with high lifetime revenue and recent activity
- customers with high lifetime revenue but older last rental dates
- differences between rental count and average payment size
- whether the highest-value customers are concentrated in one store

## Why this matters in an interview

This project lets me explain customer-level grain, revenue reconciliation, and
why I calculate recency relative to the dataset instead of the current date. It
also shows that I avoid claiming margin or profit when the dataset only supports
gross revenue.
